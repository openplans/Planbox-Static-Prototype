▱ Plan In A Box

One-click Promotion for Street Improvement Projects

"What's going on here?" – Citizens

Your planning project is damaged by people not having the right info. Plan In A Box gets your project information out into the world, making it easier for people to discover and stay informed.

Planners & City Officials,
Help shape Plan In A Box by signing up to be an early user and telling us about the biggest challenge you face when sharing your project information.

Information about proposed street changes is often hard to find and track.

Sharing timely, helpful information is challenging for busy planners.

Being uninformed leads to frustration and unsatisfactory experiences, even for great projects. And unhappy people slow down the public process for everyone.

Plan In A Box helps planners create and share information about projects, so neighbors can stay informed about proposed changes.

OpenPlans is developing Plan In A Box with support from the Knight Foundation. We're just getting started, stay tuned for a beta soon!
